Characters Personality
Ula Nara Qingying/Ula Nara Ruyi/Lady Qing/Imperial Noble Consort Xian/the Step-Empress 100% Heroism Rating: Everyone who gets to know Ruyi loves her kindness towards others. Many people are loyal to her because of this like Hailan, Xiangjian, Meiruo, Rongpei, Sanbao, Suoxin, etc. Even Hongli's servants Yuhu and Li Yu see how good she is (Ruyi is cordial to Yuhu and Ruyi treated Li Yu's wounds). Of course, there are people who don't like Ruyi but there are mostly the antagonists who want power and want to ruin Ruyi. All-Loving Hero: From the beginning to the end, she was a truly sincere, good woman in a place where power conflicts and corruption boils. When she dies unlike the funeral for Empress Fuca, where everyone pretended to mourn her, people who knew Ruyi mourns her death deeply with consorts and servants who knew her being there crying at her funeral.

Even finding out about everything, she orders everyone from her palace to leave for a bit so she can set her palace on fire without killing anyone but herself. Even as a pregnant Ruyi tries to save her, the doors are locked so Yihuan dies in the fire.
